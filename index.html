Admissions in msrit for UG cources

Candidates must pass 10th and 12th with physics and mathematics as compulsory subjects and cs/bio/electronics as optional. Entrance exam: KCET or COEDK

What is the grading system?

Absolute grading system with scale of 10.Marks above 90% is given A. marks above 80%given b.Marks above 70% is given C.Marks above 60% is given D.Marks above 50% is given E .Marks below 50% is given F.
